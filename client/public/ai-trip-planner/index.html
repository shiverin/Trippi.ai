What an AI trip planner should actually do

A useful travel planner keeps decisions, context, and next steps together. Trippi focuses on the messy parts of group travel: choosing places, sequencing days, tracking costs, keeping files handy, and turning scattered ideas into a trip people can follow.

Itineraries with context

Plan days around places, notes, reservations, travel time, and route decisions instead of isolated text.

Shared travel workspace

Invite collaborators, keep edits visible, and use one plan as the source of truth for the group.

Budgets and documents

Keep costs, tickets, PDFs, packing notes, and booking details beside the trip they belong to.

Can I use Trippi for a group trip?
