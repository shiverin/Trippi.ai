When to use an itinerary generator

Trippi is useful when you know the destination but need a workable plan, or when your group has too many ideas and needs a shared structure.

  • Draft a weekend itinerary, city break, road trip, family trip, or team retreat.
  • Compare places and routes before committing to a schedule.
  • Store tickets, booking confirmations, and travel notes in the same workspace.
  • Keep the itinerary private until you choose who can view or edit it.

Itinerary generator FAQ

Can I edit the generated itinerary?

Yes. Trippi is meant to be a living travel workspace, so the generated plan can change as the trip becomes more real.

Does Trippi include budgets and reservations?

Trippi includes shared budgeting, reservation context, trip files, maps, and routes alongside the itinerary.
