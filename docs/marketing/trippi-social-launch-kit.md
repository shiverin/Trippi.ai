# Trippi Social Launch Kit

Purpose: set up TikTok and Instagram so Trippi feels active, fun, useful, and close to launch before broader launch pushes.

Primary URL: https://www.trippi.lol/

Core line: Trippi.ai, your Trippi, our Troppa.

Support lines:

- Plan smarter.
- Less confusion, more fun.
- Turn the group chat into a trip.

## Launch Positioning

### Audience

- Friends planning group trips.
- The one person who always becomes the trip organizer.
- AI-curious travelers who want a plan, not another spreadsheet.
- Student groups, friend groups, couples trips, families, and small teams.

### Promise

Trippi turns messy travel planning into one shared AI trip planner with itineraries, maps, budgets, reservations, files, and routes.

### Campaign Angle

Use:

`Trippi.ai, your Trippi, our Troppa.`

Meaning:

- `Your Trippi`: every traveler gets a smarter, personal trip plan.
- `Our Troppa`: the mascot brings the chaotic brainrot energy, making planning feel fun instead of stressful.
- `Plan smarter`: the practical promise.
- `Less confusion, more fun`: the emotional promise.

Short version for profile headers:

`Plan smarter. Less confusion, more fun.`

Short version for video endings:

`your Trippi. our Troppa.`

### Personality

- Helpful but slightly chaotic in a relatable way.
- Fast, visual, and social-first.
- More "this solves the group chat problem" than "enterprise AI workflow."
- Fun enough for TikTok, credible enough for founders and early adopters.
- Brainrot-aware without becoming unreadable.
- Weird, playful, and useful in that order for TikTok.

### Do Not Say

- Do not promise automatic booking or guaranteed prices.
- Do not imply Trippi spends money, books flights, or changes plans without user permission.
- Do not call it the only AI trip planner. Say "built for group trips" instead.
- Do not use another creator's exact mascot art as the official brand asset unless you have permission.

- Publish map problem post.

- Publish before/after post.

- Ask viewers to follow, comment, and send the account to the trip organizer friend.

## Comment Strategy

Reply style:

- Short, specific, useful.
- Ask one follow-up question.
- Avoid sounding like a bot.

Examples:

- "Tokyo is perfect for this. How many days and what's the budget vibe?"
- "Group trips always break at the budget step. That's exactly why we're building this."
- "Stealing this as a demo prompt. Beach trip or city trip?"
- "The itinerary is only half the problem. The other half is keeping everyone aligned."

## First DM Reply

Thanks for checking out Trippi. We're launching soon. What kind of trip are you trying to plan?

If they answer:

Ask for destination, dates, group size, budget, and vibe. Offer to turn one into a demo.
